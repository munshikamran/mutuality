About Us

The Story

Last year, we moved to Seattle from the east coast. Upon arrival, Jasjit was on the prowl for some poker, Kamran for a gym buddy, and Jeff was well, just on the prowl. Even though the summer was warmer than expected the social life was chillier. Like many, we found "meet-people sites" lacking or just really creepy. We knew the best way to meet cool people "offline" was through mutual friends. So we started Mutuality to digitize the hell out of this obvious truth.

Stay engaged through our facebook, our tweets, and our blog. Also please email us your thoughts at info@mymutuality.com.
