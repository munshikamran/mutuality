About Us

The Story

Last year we moved to Seattle from the east coast. Even though the summer was warmer than expected, the social life was chillier. Like many others we found traditional people meeting sites lacking or just really creepy. We figured the best way to meet cool people online would be the same way we met cool people in real life - through mutual friends. So we started Mutuality to do just that.

Stay engaged through our facebook page, our tweets, and our blog. Feel free to email us at info@mymutuality.com.
