About Us

The Story

Last year, we moved to Seattle from the east coast. Even though the summer was warmer than expected the social life was chillier. Like many, we found "meet-people sites" lacking or just really creepy. We knew the best way to meet cool people "offline" was through mutual friends. So we started Mutuality to digitize the heck out of this obvious truth.

Stay engaged through our facebook, our tweets, and our blog. Please do email us your thoughts at info@mymutuality.com.
